About Us

From show-stopping venues to oh-wow bao buns to the ice cream at the end of the night, we do it all - and here’s our story.

Introducing Dignans Catering

Established five years ago in Edinburgh, Dignans is a high-quality outside catering company that operates from their café/brasserie. Known for serving local businesses and offices with regular catering contracts and one-off celebration buffets, Dignans has built its reputation through word-of-mouth recommendations and minimal advertising via their company vehicle, business cards, and local directories. Looking to expand their reach within the Edinburgh district, the company aims to delve into office lunches, corporate catering, and corporate barbecues. To achieve this, the owners have decided to create an attractive, user-friendly website that showcases their delicious menus and allows easy online ordering for simple or repeat catering needs. This new platform will also feature monthly special offers and be optimized for search engines to ensure potential customers can easily find them. While they have a basic logo on their printed menus, they are open to revamping it to better suit their new online presence.

Our Catering Services
